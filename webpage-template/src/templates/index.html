Endangered Avian Species

A National Wildlife Refuge (NWR) is a protected area in United States for endangered wildlife. In this interactave map, you can see how many endangered bird species are in each State, the species name, and its protected area. The Layer Views for Endangered Birds displays a grouping for each State. Click the group to expand it for identifying the individual species.

Endangered Foliage

Humans and animals have an impact on plant life simply because they rely on it for mere existence. Consequently, overpopulation can endanger plant species. There are other protected areas such as Wetland Managment Districts (WMD) that focus on conservation of threatend or endangered plants. Expand the Endangered Plants group on its Layer View to see the names of the plant species.
